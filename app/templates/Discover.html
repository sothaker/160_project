Meditate

Meditation, which is the practice of focused concentration, bringing yourself back to the moment over and over again, actually addresses stress, whether positive or negative.

Pranayamic Breathing

There are various techniques that can mitigate anxiety and stress. Shitali pranayama, which is a cooling form of pranyama, is great for calming the mind and body, resulting in lower hypertension and relaxed nerves.

Journal

Journaling also helps you better organize your thoughts. According to the University of Rochester Medical Center, "When you have a problem, and you're stressed, keeping a journal can help you identify what's causing that stress or anxiety. Then, once you've identified your stressors, you can work on a plan to resolve the problems and, in turn, reduce stress."
